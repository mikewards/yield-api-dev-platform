## 🌐 Make It Public (Optional)

### Deploy Frontend to Netlify (5 minutes)

1. Go to [netlify.com](https://netlify.com)
2. Sign up/login (free)
3. Click **"Add new site"** → **"Deploy manually"**
4. Drag and drop your **P1 folder**
5. Wait ~30 seconds
6. You'll get a URL like: `https://random-name-123.netlify.app`
7. Share with friends! 🎉

### Or Deploy to Vercel

1. Go to [vercel.com](https://vercel.com)
2. Sign up/login (free)
3. Click **"Add New Project"**
4. Import your GitHub repo: `wardmic4/flow-platform`
5. Root directory: `/` (root)
6. Deploy!
7. You'll get a URL like: `https://flow-platform.vercel.app`
